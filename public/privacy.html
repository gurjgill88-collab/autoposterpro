Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

AutoPosterPro ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Chrome extension and related services.

1. Information We Collect

License Information: When you activate a license, we collect your license key and a unique device identifier to verify your subscription and prevent unauthorized use.

Dealer Information: You may optionally provide your dealership name and dealer registry number, which is stored locally and used in vehicle listings.

Usage Data: We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve our service, such as feature usage frequency and error reports.

Vehicle Data: Vehicle information you scrape from dealer websites is processed locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To verify and manage your software license
  • To provide customer support
  • To improve our products and services
  • To communicate with you about updates and changes

3. Data Storage and Security

Your license data is stored securely on our servers. Vehicle data and settings are stored locally on your device using Chrome's secure storage APIs. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information.

4. Third-Party Services

Our extension interacts with Facebook Marketplace to post listings on your behalf. We do not share your data with Facebook beyond what is necessary to create your listings. If you enable AI descriptions, vehicle data is sent to Google's Gemini API to generate descriptions.

5. Data Retention

We retain your license information for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your data by contacting us.

6. Your Rights

For Canadian Residents (PIPEDA): You have the right to access, correct, and delete your personal information. You may withdraw consent at any time.

For US Residents (CCPA): California residents have the right to know what personal information is collected, request deletion, and opt-out of sale of personal information. We do not sell your personal information.

For All Users: You may deactivate your license at any time through the extension settings, which removes your device association from our servers.

7. Children's Privacy

Our service is intended for business use by adults. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13 (or 16 in some jurisdictions).

8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy on our website.

10. Contact Us
