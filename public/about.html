Our Story

AutoPosterPro was born from a simple observation: car salespeople are great at selling cars, but they shouldn't have to spend their valuable time copying and pasting vehicle information into Facebook Marketplace over and over again.

We partnered with dealerships across Canada to understand their workflow and built a tool that takes the tedious work out of social selling. What used to take 15 minutes per listing now takes seconds.
